About Me

Hello there, I am zyenapz! I'm a college sophomore pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science. In addition, I'm an aspiring game developer and pixel artist who lives in Laguna, Philippines. I also make modifications (mods) for various games such as Counter-Strike, Ace of Spades, and the early Doom titles. I am active on itch.io and have currently published 2 games on the platform, those being: Star Fighter and Yukon Escape.

I also write blogs detailing development processes, my learnings, and sometimes, blog posts that are random. Anyways, thank you for checking out my website and of course, please enjoy your stay!
